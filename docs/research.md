## Publications

### Ontological Knowledge Blocks: Executable, Profile-Based Compliance for Trustworthy AI Services

This paper introduces the concept of Ontological Knowledge Blocks, a programmable governance infrastructure that compiles regulatory obligations into machine-checkable SHACL constraints over RDF evidence graphs. The work provides the theoretical and technical foundation for the [EK-BLOCKS](https://github.com/Open-Systems-Collective/ek-blocks) prototype.
