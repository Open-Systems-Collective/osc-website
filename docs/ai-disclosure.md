# AI-Assisted Development

The Open Systems Collective uses AI-assisted development tooling (Claude Code) for infrastructure setup, documentation scaffolding, and code generation across its repositories.

All research design, regulatory interpretation, ontological modelling, and architectural decisions are made by human contributors. AI tooling is used under human direction and review.

This practice is consistent with the transparency and accountability principles that our Open Knowledge Blocks framework advocates. We believe that AI assistance in research should be disclosed openly, not hidden.

This disclosure follows the recommendations of major scientific publishers regarding AI assistance in research.
